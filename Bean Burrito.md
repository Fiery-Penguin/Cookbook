## Ingredients
___
**Base**
- [ ] 2 Cans of Kidney Beans
- [ ] 1 Can og chopped Tomatoes
- [ ] 1 Large onion, finely diced
- [ ] 1 Eggplant (or Squash), coarsely diced
- [ ] 200 g uncooked rice
- [ ] 2 veggie stock cubes 

**Nice additions**
- [ ] 100 g Button Mushrooms (Gives Umami and volume)
- [ ] 1 Large Carrot, finely grated (Gives sweetness and volume)
## Walkthrough
___
