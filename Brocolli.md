#Italian #Pizza #Vegetarian 
[[Pizza Toppings|Return]] 
## Ingredients (1 pizza)
___
- [ ] 1 [[Sourdough Pizza|Pizza dough]]
- [ ] 1/2 Can of Pesto
- [ ] Small slices of broccoli
- [ ] 40 g Mozzarella (low moisture if you're *bougie*)
- [ ] 200 g Feta cubes
- [ ] Garlic oil
## Walkthrough
___
**Preperations**
1. Set the oven to 275$\degree$C with a stone 1/4 highest rack for 2-3 hours to heat it up
2. Add garlic and salt into oil and set aside for quick garlic oil

**Baking**
1. Spread out the pizza-dough on parchment paper and add Pesto, broccoli, mozzarella and feta cubes to the pizza.
2. Slide the pizza into the oven and let bake until crust starts browning, remove and put on wire rack to keep the bottom crispy.
