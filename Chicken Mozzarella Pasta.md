#SoMe 
## Ingredients (4 people)
___
- [ ] 125 g Mozzarella 
- [ ] 100 g philidenphia 
- [ ] 400 g chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 500 g chicken bits
- [ ] 400 g dried pasta, boiled
- [ ] 200 g frozen spinach, thawed
- [ ] 1 Large Onion
- [ ] 4 Cloves of garlic
- [ ] Paprika
- [ ] Chilli flakes
## Walkthrough
___
1. Fry garlic in oil with spices till golden, chicken and cook it together till it's done, set aside
2. Add thawed spinach to pan with mozzarella cream cheese and canned tomato, 
3. Once cheese is melted, add back the chicken and the cooked pasta and chopped onions to the pan, combine it and serve with parmesan.
