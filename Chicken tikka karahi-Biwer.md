#Indian #Chicken
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
**Chicken**
- [ ] 500 g Chicken
- [ ] Lemon Juice
- [ ] 2 Tsp Kashmiri chilli powder (not spicy)
- [ ] 1 Tsp Salt
- [ ] 1/4 Tsp Turmeric
- [ ] 1 Tsp Coriander
- [ ] 1 Tsp Garam Masala
- [ ] 1 Tsp Red Chilli powder

**Sauce**
- [ ] 1 Large Onion, chopped
- [ ] 1 Cup Yoghurt
- [ ] 1/2 Canned Tomato (Or 4 large tomatoes)
- [ ] 1 Tsp Chaat Masala (Can be substituted with Garam Masala)
- [x] 2 Tsp Red Chilli powder
- [x] 2 Tsp Black Pepper
- [x] 2 Tsp Ground Cumin
- [x] 2 Tsp ground Coriander
- [ ] 1 Tsp ginger
- [x] 1 Tsp Salt

**Additional**
- [ ] [[Jeera rice]]
- [ ] thin strips of ginger
- [ ] coarsely chopped cilantro
## Walkthrough
___
1. Start making [[Jeera rice]] 
2. Rub oil, lemon and marinade spices into chicken and let rest for 10 minutes
3. Heat up pan with oil for shallow frying on medium high and add chicken, let sit and keep an eye on it while moving along
4. On a second pan, heat up oil and sauté onions until slightly browned, add ginger, sauté for a little and then add spices, Once nice and fragrant, add yoghurt and tomatoes, mix and add cilantro and ginger.
5. Once the chicken is done, kill the heat and add the chicken to the rest of the dish
6. Add a lid and let the dish simmer on low heat for 10 minutes, add fresh cilantro and ginger once finished and serve with the [[Jeera rice]]
