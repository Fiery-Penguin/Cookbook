#Vegetarian #Recipe/Spanish 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 2 onions, chopped coarsely
- [ ] 30 g Dark Chocolate
- [ ] 1 Aubergine, blended
- [ ] 1 Squash, blended
- [ ] 2 cans of Kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- [ ] 1 can of chopped Tomato
- [ ] 1 dl water with two stock cubes
- [ ] 250 g rice (weighed before cooking)
- [ ] 100 g Low Fat Greek Yoghurt
- [ ] Cilantro 
- [ ] Lime 
- [ ] 1.5 Tsp ground cumin
- [ ] 1.5 Tsp ground Koriander
- [ ] 1 Tsp Cinnamon
- [ ] 1 Tsp Paprika
- [ ] 0.5 Tsp Chilli flakes
- [ ] 2 cloves of Garlic
## Walkthrough
___
1. Add spices to a pan without oil and fry for a short time until fragrant to roast the spices
1. Add oil, onions and garlic to the pan, stir and fry them until the onions are soft, add the aubergine, squash and chopped tomatoes along with the bouillon. Let simmer for about 30 minutes
2. Add the beans and let them simmer along with the dish for an additional 5 minutes
3. Once done, add the chocolate, stir it well into the dish so as the colour is completely homogeneous, taste test and check for the need of additional salt or spice (most likely spice)
4. Serve over rice with some Greek yoghurt, lime and a good handful of cilantro
