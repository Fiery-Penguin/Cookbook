[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients (for 2 people) 
___
- [ ] 1 Egg
- [ ] 2 dl Flour (140 g)
- [ ] 3 dl Milk
- [ ] 2 tsp dessert MSG
## Walkthrough
___
1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
2. Heat up can to middle high
3. Between each pour, add 1/2 tsp of butter to pan
4. Pour batter into pan
5. Heat on both sides
6. Let rest under a cloth or tinfoil (cringe) to capture moisture and keep the pancakes warm
