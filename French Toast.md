#Dessert #Egg #Sourdough 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 4 Slices of [[Sourdough Bread]] (1 cm thickness)
- [ ] 2 Large Eggs
- [ ] 2 Tbsp Sugar
- [ ] 0.5 Tsp Cinnamon
## Walkthrough
___
1. Mix eggs, sugar and cinnamon in a bowl and whisk until homogeneous 
1. Heat up a skillet to medium heat and add butter
2. Coat 2 pieces of bread at a time and add them to the pan while adjusting heat as necessary. When small amounts of smoke starts coming from under the bread, flip and wait again.
3. Once the toast is fried on both sides, take off heat and place on a plate ready for serving.
