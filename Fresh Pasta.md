#Italian #Flour #Egg 
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients (300 g Pasta)
___
- [ ] 2 Eggs (120g)
- [ ] 200 g All purpose flour
## Walkthrough
___
**Preparation**
1. Mix together eggs and flour as well as possible
2. Once too hard to mix, knead for about 8-10 minutes
3. Set aside covered for 20 minutes to rest
4. Roll out into a flat sheet, dusts well with flour and fold unto self
5. Cut strips of the desired width and separate the individual strips

**Boiling**
1. Place into boiling water for a few minutes
2. Once boiled, move pasta directly from water to dish without rinsing[^2]
3. Discard water
