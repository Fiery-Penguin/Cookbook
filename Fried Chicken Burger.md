#Unhealthy #Chicken
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (2 Burgers)
___
**Chicken**
- [ ] 1 Chicken Breast
- [ ] 1 Egg
- [ ] 100g Milk
- [ ] 25g [[Pickled Cucumber|Pickle Juice]]
- [ ] Pepper
- [ ] Salt

**Breading**
- [ ] 75g Flour
- [ ] 35g Potato-starch (because corn-starch is american and cringe)
- [ ] 5g Paprika
- [ ] 5g Onion powder
- [ ] 5g Corriander
- [ ] 3g Cayenne
- [ ] 3g Pepper
- [ ] Salt to taste

**Sauce**
- [ ] 3 Parts [[Mayonnaise]]
- [ ] ¼ Parts [[Pickled Cucumber|Pickle Juice]]
- [ ] 1 Part Dijon Mustard
- [ ] ¼ Parts Honey
- [ ] Salt to taste

**Misc & Toppings**
- [ ] Burger buns of choice
- [ ] [[Pickled Cucumber]]
- [ ] Ice-berg, shredded (optional)
- [ ] Tomato, sliced (optional)
- [ ] Red-onion, sliced (optional)
- [ ] Cheese (optional)

## Walkthrough
___
**Preparation**
1. Take the chicken and beat it flat with what ever means possible, when flat, cut into 2 roughly equal pieces, set aside.
2. Bring all ingredients for the marinade together in a bowl, whisk well to incorporate completely, add the chicken to the marinade and set aside.
3. Bring all ingredients for the flour mixture together and whisk well to combine everything.
4. Bring together Mayo, Dijon mustard, honey and pickle juice in a vessel and whisk to combine, add salt to taste *(Mayo is plenty salty already, so don't overdo it)*.
5. Add oil to a pan or pot, large enough to fry one piece of chicken at a time, start heating the oil and keep an eye on it to make sure it doesn't overheat.

**Frying**

1. Once the oil is hot, add a piece of chicken to the flour, make sure to get flour into every part of the chicken and then lay the chicken gently into the oil. After a little bit, flip and let the other side fry, once its slightly golden with minimum browning, remove the chicken and let cool on a wire-rack, repeat for every piece of chicken. *(If you want the chicken extra crispy, redo this for the already fried pieces after they're all done with the first fry, this removes any moisture that has escaped into the breading and gives a better result in the end)*.
2. Once done, heat the burger buns in the oven or on a pan, Add the sauce to both top and bottom bun, add burger vegetables of choice, as well as the pickled cucumbers and of course, the chicken..
3. All done!
