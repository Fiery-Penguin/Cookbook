#Vegetarian #Danish #Egg #Classic
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 500g Pea Protein Mince
- [ ] 2 Tsp Flour
- [ ] 4 Tsp Oats
- [ ] 1 dl Milk
- [ ] 2 Eggs (120g)
- [ ] 1 Onion (roughly grated)
- [ ] 1 Garlic Glove
- [ ] 1 Tsp Thyme
- [ ] 0.5 Tsp Cumin
- [ ] 2 Tsp Salt
- [ ] Freshly Cracked Pepper
## Walkthrough
___
1. Mix all ingredients together until relatively homogeneous.
2. If too sticky(?), but in fridge for 30 minutes
3. Shape into balls and grill for around 5 minutes on either side

> [!info] 
> Pea Protein Mince can be substituted with minced pork
