#Yeast #Flour #Butter
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (30 Stk)
___
- [ ] 50 g Yeast
- [ ] 7.5 dl Full-fat Milk
- [ ] 2.5 Tsp salt
- [ ] 2.5 Tbsp Sugar
- [ ] 3.75 Tsp Cardamom
- [ ] 2.5 Egg
- [ ] 187.5 g Butter
- [ ] 125 g Wholegrain Wheat Flour
- [ ] 1175 g All-Purpose flour

**For egg-washing**
- [ ] 2.5 Egg
## Walkthrough
___
1. Mix yeast and yeast in a bowl, add the all other ingredients together thoroughly for 10 minutes.
2. Let the dough ferment for an hour on the kitchen table.
3. Part the dough into equal sizes and shape them completely round. place them on a baking tray lined with parchment and let them once again rest for 45 minutes
4. Brush the buns with a beat egg and bake them in a 190$\degree$C oven for 15-17 minutes.
5. Let the buns cool down on a wire-rack before serving.
