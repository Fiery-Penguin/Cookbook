#Drink #Gin #Mango
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 4 cl Gin
- [ ] 2 cl Mango Syrup
- [ ] 3 Ice cubes
- [ ] Lemon Soda
## Walkthrough
___
1. Add gin, syrup and ice cubes to a glass
2. Fill the glass with lemon soda and stir
3. Enjoy


> [!info] Info
> - Add 1/2 cl of lemon juice for a fresher taste
