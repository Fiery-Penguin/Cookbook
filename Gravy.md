#Sauce #Danish 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (350 g)
___
- [ ] 20 g Butter
- [ ] 30 g Flour
- [ ] 300 g Milk
## Walkthrough
___
1. Mix together hot butter and flour
2. Once completely mixed, pour in very little amounts of milk (30 g per addition) and completely homogenize before adding more (Be patient with this step or else you **will** get clumps)
3. Once the gravy is very liquid and no clumps will occur, add the rest of the milk
4. Add here the flavours you want, like bouillon, salt, pepper, nutmeg, beef-fond (for brown sauce) ect.
5. Turn up the heat when the dish is about time to thicken the gravy.
6. Serve warm
