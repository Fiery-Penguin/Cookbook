#Beverage #Chocolate #Sweet
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (1 Serving)
___
- [ ] 50 g [[Chocolate-Powder]]
- [ ] 250 g 1.5% Fat milk
## Walkthrough
___
1. Heat up the milk in a pot just until it starts foaming, stirring constantly to prevent burning
2. Once the milk is hot, kill the heat and whisk in the [[Chocolate-Powder]] until it's completely absolved
3. Serve possibly with some whipped cream and a sprinkle of the powder for aesthetic purposes
