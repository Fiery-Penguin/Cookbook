#Refreshing
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 1L water
- [ ] 70g Sugar
- [ ] 1 Lemon (Juiced)
- [ ] 10 bags of cheap tea[^1]
## Walkthrough
___
1. Bring 1/5 of the water to a boil and pour it on a pot.
2. Add sugar to the pot and stir till it's completely dissolved.
3. Add the remaining water to the pot, wondering the temperature before adding the lemon juice.
4. Pour the mixture into a container, with the tea-bags, close the container and let rest for about 8 hours in the fridge.
5. Discard bags and keep stored cool (lasts about a maximum of one week).

*Last amendment: 2024-06-25* 

[^1]: Trust me, with this many bags, you'll want it to be cheap
