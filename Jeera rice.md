#Indian 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (For 2 people)
___
- [ ] 180g rice
- [ ] 1/2 Tsp Cumin seed or ground cumin
- [ ] 2g salt
## Walkthrough
___
1. Heat oil in a pan and heat it to medium high
2. Add cumin and stir until fregrant
3. Add rice to pan and fry the rice for a minute or so
4. Add equal parts water to the weight of the rice and bright to a light simmer
5. Once done, fluff the rice to prevent the rice from lumping together
