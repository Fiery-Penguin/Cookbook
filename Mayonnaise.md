#Condiment #Egg 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (twice what you need)
___
- [ ] 200g oil
- [ ] 1 egg (60g)
- [ ] 10g mustard
- [ ] 10g vinager
- [ ] 4g salt
- [ ] 2g sugar
## Walkthrough
___
1. Add all the ingredients except for the oil to a cylindrical container about the width of your immersion blender[^1] and quickly blend them to combine.
2. Add the oil such that the ingredients are all at the bottom and the oil on top
3. Move immersion blender to the bottom of the container and start blending. Move slowly moving up through the oil to emulsify the eggs and oil
4. Once there are no clear streaks of oil, remove the immersion blender and enjoy
