#Vegetarian 
## Ingredients (2 large burgers)
___
- [ ] 2 Buns, cut through
- [ ] [[Mayonnaise]]
- [ ] Kethup
- [ ] Tomatoes
- [ ] Iceberg salat
- [ ] Cheese
- [ ] Pickled Cucumber

**Patty**
- [ ] 125g+ Button mushrooms
- [ ] 50g flour 
- [ ] 50g oats
- [ ] 1 Egg (60g)

**Oven fries**
- [ ] Potatoes
- [ ] Thyme
- [ ] Paprika
- [ ] Onion powder
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Pepper
## Walkthrough
___
1. Blend the mushrooms so they're tiny bits
2. Add an egg to a bowl, whisk egg(s) to homogenise and add mushrooms, oats and flour along with a small teaspoon of salt.
3. Mix all till homogeneous and set aside covered
4. Heat up oil in a pan, once hot, place  balls of the mushroom patty in the oil and press flat with a lightly oiled skillet to prevent sticking.
5. Once crispy on the bottom, flip and add cheese to the top while on the pan. Once crispy on both sides they're done.
