#Sourdough #Bread #Flour 
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients (2x230 g Buns)
___
- [ ] 200 g Wheat flour
- [ ] 50 g Rye flour
- [ ] 200 g Water (80%)
- [ ] 6 g Dried Yeast
- [ ] 5 g Salt (2%)
## Walkthrough
___
**Preparation**
1. Mix water, starter and salt in a bowl
2. Add flour and mix until decently combined
3. Rest for 30 min
4. Fold and part into separate buns, place in burger forms and let rise
5. Once risen for 1.5 hours, heat oven to 250C and bake until top turns brown
