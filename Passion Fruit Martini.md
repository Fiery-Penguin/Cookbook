#Drink #Vodka 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 4 cl Vodka
- [ ] 4 cl Passion Fruit Syrup
- [ ] 2 cl fresh lime juice
- [ ] 1/2 Passion fruit
## Walkthrough
___
1. Add vodka, passion fruit syrup and fresh lime juice to a shaker
2. Shake for 10 seconds and sift into a martini glass, decorate with sliced passion fruit
