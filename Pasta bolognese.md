## Ingredients
___
- [x] 400g [[Fresh Pasta|Pasta]]
- [x] 250g Bacon, cubes
- [x] 2 Onions, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 sticks of celery, roughly chopped
- [x] 2 Cloves of garlic
- [x] 2 Sticks of fresh thyme (or 2 tsp dried)
- [x] 500g minced beef
- [x] 2 Cans chopped tomatoes
- [x] 1 Tsp Marmite
- [x] Parmigiano Reggiano or Grana Padano
- [x] Salt and pepper

## Walkthrough
___
1. Cook the bacon and set aside
2. in the bacon fat, cook onions and thyme, add garlic when the onions are soft
3. Shortly after adding the garlic, add the meat to the pan and let it brown well before adding the tomatoes and the cooked bacon
4. Put on a lid and let simmer on low heat for up to an hour, keep in mind that it still needs about 30 min to remove most of the moisture
5. Fresh pasta cooks quickly, so when the sauce is about 5 minutes from being done, put the pasta in boiling water and let it boil for about 2-3 minutes
6. Discard the pasta water and add a little oil to the pasta to keep it from sticking. When the source is done, taste it and add salt and pepper as needed
7. Serve with Parmigiano Reggiano or Grana Padano
 
>[!info] Tips
> - Recipe originally from Valdemarsro, to make the original recipe, add 1dl of red wine, 2 Tsp balsamico and 100g of sundried tomatoes lower bacon to 50g and exclude marmite and celery
> - Marmite adds some tang and umami. highly recommended 
> - To add volume and save money, maybe add mushrooms, carrots and other veggies
> - For more environmentally friendly dishes, see [[vegetarian bolognese]]
> - Obviously, if you don't use [[fresh pasta]], cook the pasta for longer until it feels done when you eat it
