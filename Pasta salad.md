#Vegetarian 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] Dried pasta screws
- [ ] 1/3 Jar pesto
- [ ] 1 Block of feta
- [ ] 1 Can of Chickpeas
- [ ] 1 Cucumber
- [ ] 1/2 Bucket of cherry tomatoes
- [ ] 1/3 Cabbage
## Walkthrough
___
1. Boil pasta till done and dump in ice water or keep under running water to cool off
2. Rinse chickpeas under running water
3. Chop cucumber, cherry tomatoes and feta into small bits
4. Add everything to a bowl with pesto and mix together
5. Enjoy
