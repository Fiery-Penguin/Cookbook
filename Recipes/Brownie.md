#Dessert #Chocolate
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients (for 413 cm$^2$ pan)
___
- [ ] 113 g Butter
- [ ] 1 Tsp Oil
- [ ] 225 g Sugar
- [ ] 2 Eggs
- [ ] 2 tsp Dessert MSG
- [ ] 63 g All purpose flour
- [ ] 63 g Cocoa powder
- [ ] 1.4 g salt
## Walkthrough
___
1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
2. Lightly grease a baking pan with cooking oil. Line with parchment paper (or baking paper); set aside.
3. Combine hot melted butter, oil and sugar together in a medium-sized bowl. Whisk well for about a minute. Add the eggs and vanilla; beat until lighter in colour (another minute).
4. Sift in flour, cocoa powder and salt. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until JUST combined (do NOT over beat as doing so well affect the texture of your brownies).
6. Pour batter into prepared pan, smoothing the top out evenly. (OPTIONAL: Top with chocolate chunks or chocolate chips.)
6. Bake for 27-33 minutes, or until the centre of the brownies in the pan no longer jiggles and is just set to the touch (the brownies will keep baking in the hot pan out of the oven). If testing with a toothpick, the toothpick should come out dirty for fudge-textured brownies. Alternatively open and jiggle the dough around the 25 min mark to see if it's firm or not
7. Remove and allow to cool to room temperature before slicing into 12 brownies.
