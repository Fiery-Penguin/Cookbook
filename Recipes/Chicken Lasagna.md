#Chicken 
## Ingredients (2 Hungry people)
___
- [ ] 450 g Ground Chicken
- [ ] 2 Onions, finely chopped
- [ ] 3 Cloves of Garlic, grated
- [ ] 1 Aubergine, roughly blended
- [ ] 1 Can of chopped tomato
- [ ] 70 g og tomato Puré
- [ ] 500 mL Mornay Sauce
- [ ] 500 g of Water
- [ ] 2 Chicken Stock Cubes
- [ ] Spinach
## Walkthrough
___
**Sauce**
1. Start preheating the oven to 200$\degree C$
2. To a hot pan with oil, add onions and then them turn golden, Add the chicken and it it slightly brown.
3. Add then Aubergine, Garlic, stir for a little bit and pour in the canned-tomato, puré, stock cubes and water. Let this simmer on medium heat for however long you feel like.
4. Once done, kill the heat and add spinach[^1] to the dish.

**Construction**
1. Build the lasagna, starting with lubricating the bottom, adding a light layer of Mornay, a light layer of the sauce, and then doing plate, sauce, Mornay for as many layers as are possible, finish with a thick layer of Mornay and put it into the oven for 30 minutes.
2. Once done, remove from heat and enjoy
