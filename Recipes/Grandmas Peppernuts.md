#Dessert
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 125 g Butter
- [ ] 125 g Sugar
- [ ] 1 Egg
- [ ] 1 Tsp Natron
- [ ] ½ Tsp Ginger
- [ ] ¾ Tsp Cardamom
- [ ] ½ Tsp Cinnamon
- [ ] 1 Knifetip white pepper (ground)
- [ ] 275 g Flour
## Walkthrough
___
1. Mix together Sugar and butter, add the egg and mix all together
2. Add spices and natron into the flour and mix the flour and knead everything together
3. Roll into small spheres (1 cm in diameter) and place on a tray lined with parchment paper
4. Bake at 200$\degree$C for 10-12 minutes
