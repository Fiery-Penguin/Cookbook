#Asian 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 250 g minced meat
- [ ] 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- [ ] 2 gloves of garlic
- [ ] Cooked white rice 
- [ ] spring onions
## Walkthrough
___
1. Add meat to a hot pan with oil and cook until browned and slightly dehydrated
2. Add soy sauce and garlic and stir around until the soy sauce has reduced and coated the meat
3. Serve over cooked rice and garnish with spring onions
