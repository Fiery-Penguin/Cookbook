#Vegetarian 
## Ingredients (For 2 people)
___
- [ ] 1 Onion, Coarsely chopped
- [ ] 3 Cloves of garlic, Finely chopped
- [ ] 200 g Potatoes, Chopped in large bits
- [ ] 1 Can of chopped Tomato
- [ ] 1 Can of Coconut Milk
- [ ] 200 g Red Lentils, Washed under running water
- [ ] 1 Knob of ginger, Finely sliced
- [ ] 1 Tsp Curry
- [ ] 1 Tsp Cumin
- [ ] 1 Tsp Turmeric
- [ ] 1/2 Tsp Koriander
- [ ] 2 Vegetable stock cubes
## Walkthrough
___
1. Add onion and garlic to a pot with hot oil and saute, Add spices and stir them around to prevent burning
2. Once fragrant, add the lentils, mix everything together and add the potato, tomato, stock cubes and coconut milk. Let the dish simmer until the potatoes are no longer raw while stiring occasionally to prevent burning on the bottom.
3. Serve as is, or with a type of bread on the side.
