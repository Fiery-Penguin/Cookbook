## Ingredients (1 Jar)
___
- [ ] 100 g Water
- [ ] 100 g Vinager
- [ ] 13 g Sugar
- [ ] ¼ Cucumber, sliced
## Walkthrough
___
1. Pour all ingredients into a pot and heat them up to boiling, turn off the heat
2. Add cucumber slices to a scolded heat-proof glass, along with spices of choice
3. Pour over the hot vinagar-sugar-water and close the lid, let cool before putting in freezer
