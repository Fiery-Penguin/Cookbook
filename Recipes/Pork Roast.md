#Pork
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 3 kg Pork
- [ ] Thyme
- [ ] Peppercorns
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] Bayleaf
## Walkthrough
___
1. Preheat the oven to 200$\degree$C on convection mode.
2. Turn the roast upside down in a deep oven pan, pour boiling water into the pan until the water covers the skin of the cut, but not the meat.
3. Let the pan sit in the oven for 20 minutes at 200$\degree$C convection
4. Take out the pan with the roast, turn it around so the skin faces upwards, cut lines into the skin (only the skin, don't cut into the meat). Add thyme, peppercorns and bay leafs to the water. Rub salt into the skin thoroughly.
5. Place back into the oven for how ever long the [[Pork Roast#Size and timing|table]] says at 200$\degree$C convection
6. Once done, take out and let rest for 20 minutes. Before cutting, strain the cooking liquid to use for brown sauce.
