#Sourdough #Bread #Flour 
## Ingredients (2x252 g Buns)
___
- [ ] 250 g Wheat flour
- [ ] 200 g Water (80%)
- [ ] 50 g Starter (10%)
- [ ] 5 g Salt (2%)
## Walkthrough
___
**Preparation**
1. Mix water, starter and salt in a bowl
2. Add flour and mix until decently combined
3. Let rest for 30 min
4. Fold with wet hands in bowl
5. Let rest for 30 min
6. Fold with wet hands in bowl

**Baking**
1. These buns are too soft to handle without any sort of support, part the dough into two, and place in inside two parchment lined metal rings[^1].
2. Preheat the oven to 250°C while waiting for the buns to rise (ca. 1.5 hours).
3. Place in oven for 30 minutes, after which, turn on convection to brown the top if it isn't already.
4. Take out and place on wire rack to cool before cutting.
