#Sourdough #Bread #Flour 
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 500 g Wheat flour
- [ ] 400 g Water (80%)
- [ ] 100 g Starter (10%)
- [ ] 10 g Salt (2%)
## Walkthrough
___
**Preparation**
1. Mix water, starter and salt in a bowl
2. Add flour and mix until decently combined
3. Let rest for 30 min
4. Fold with wet hands in bowl
5. Let rest for 30 min
6. Fold with wet hands in bowl
7. Place in bulk fermentation container and let rest until 50% size increase (4-6 hours, depending on environmental factors)

**Baking**
1. Set oven to 225ºC
2. Oil the sides of the container and pour into tray
3. Pour fermented dough into tray, top with salt, herbs and what ever the hearts tell you. 
4. Bake for 30 min
5. Remove from oven and let cool
