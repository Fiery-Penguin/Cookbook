#Sourdough
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (200 g)
___
- [ ] 50 g bread flour
- [ ] 50 g rye flour
- [ ] 100 g water
## Walkthrough
___
1. Add bread and rye flour to the sourdough container and mix well with a knife or fork (something long and flat is the easiest)
