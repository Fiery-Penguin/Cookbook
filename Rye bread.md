#Sourdough #Bread 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients (2 Litres)
___
- [ ] 500 g Water
- [ ] 400 g Rye Flour
- [ ] 200 g Sunflower Seeds
- [ ] 50 g [[Refreshing Sourdough Starter|Sourdough Starter]]
- [ ] 40 g Bread Flour
- [ ] 40 g Flax Seeds
- [ ] 40 g Blue Poppy Seeds
- [ ] 20 g Salt
## Walkthrough
___
**Day 1**
1. Combine all ingredients into a single bowl and mix until everything is combined *(Kneading is not possible due to the wetness of the dough)*.
2. Grease the bread pan with butter or oil *(apparently butter is better? haven't confirmed if it's true or big milk propaganda)* and pour the bread batter into the pan, making sure to level it completely, possibly add toppings like flax seeds, sunflower seeds or other types of seeds.
3. Let rest with a damp towel or lid for around 12-20 hours.

**Day 2**
1. After the dough has risen, turn on the oven at 180°C on convection mode. Bake the bread for 50 minutes until the middle of the bread has reached 96°C - 98°C.
2. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool down before cutting it out of the pan. Kan be stored in a bag or the pan once cooled off.
