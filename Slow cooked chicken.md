#Chicken #Slow-cooking
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [ ] 1 kg Chicken drumsticks
- [ ] 2 Onions, roughly slices
- [ ] 4 Cloves of Garlic, crushed
- [ ] 1 Orange worth of Juice (Optional)[^1]
- [ ] 1 Tbsp Brown sugar[^2]
- [ ] 1 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
- [ ] 5 Tsp Butter
- [ ] 3 Tsp Vegetable oil[^3]
- [ ] 2 Tsp Paprika
- [ ] 1 Tsp Dried Thyme
- [ ] 1 Tsp Dried Oregano
- [ ] 1 Tsp Garlic powder
- [ ] 1 Tsp Onion powder
- [ ] Salt and pepper (Haven't measured)
## Walkthrough
___
1. Preheat the oven to $275\degree C$ and set it to *broiler*
1.  Add everything to an oven proof dish and massage it well until everything in combined
2. Lay out the chicken in a as one large surface and place them on the top rack in the oven underneath the broiler for 15 minutes (or less, depending on the amount of charring)
3. Once done, take out of the oven, set the oven to $120 \degree C$ without *broiler*, cover the dish in tin-foil and place back into the oven for 4 hours.
4. After the 4 hours have passed, take out and serve as is or with rice and greens

Based on a the recipe from [*Cooking with Claudia*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?app=desktop&v=QcnHzpQIk1s)
