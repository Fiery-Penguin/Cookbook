#Sourdough #Bread #Flour 
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients (2 loafs)
___
- [ ] 600g Water 
- [ ] 1 kg Wheat flour 
- [ ] 100g Starter (200g in winter)
- [ ] 20g Salt
## Walkthrough
___
**Preparation**
1. Mix water, starter and salt in a bowl
2. Add flour and mix until decently combined
3. Let rest for 30 min
4. Knead or fold
5. Let rest for 30 min
6. Knead or fold
7. Place in bulk fermentation container and let rest until until 50% size increase(6-8 hours, depending on environmental factors)
8. Remove from proofing and shape into two loafs, leave loafs to rest in proofing basket for 1-2 hours (depending on environmental factors)
9. Place in fridge for baking in the morning, or bake right away (former is suggested)

**Baking**
1. Set oven to 225ºC with tray in the middle position
2. Heat water for steaming, when boiling, add to container under tray
3. Once oven is at temp, remove bread from baskets, score and place in oven
4. Bake for 30 min
5. Remove water tray, rotate the bread 180$\degree$ and bake for another 5 min
6. Remove bread and place on a wired surface to prevent soggy bread
7. Wait 1-2 hours before cutting
