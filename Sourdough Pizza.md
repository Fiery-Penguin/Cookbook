#Pizza #Sourdough #Recipe/Italian
## Ingredients (for 2x 400 g Pizzas )
___
- [ ] 423 g All purpose flour
- [ ] 283 g Water (67%)
- [ ] 84 g Starter (20%)
- [ ] 8 g Salt (2%)
## Walkthrough
___
**Preparation**
1. Mix water, starter and salt in a bowl
2. Add flour and mix until decently combined
3. Let rest for 30 min
4. Knead or fold
5. Let rest for 30 min *(optional)*
6. Knead or fold *(optional)*
7. Place in bulk fermentation container and let rest until until 50% size increase(6-8 hours, depending on environmental factors)
8. Remove from proofing and shape into two balls, oil bowls and place balls inside to rest for an hour

**Baking**
1. Set oven to highest heat setting with tray in the middle position
2. Remove balls from oiled bowls, cover in durum flour and shape into a large pizza
3. Decorate pizza with toppings of choice and add a layer of oil to the crust to make it brown in the oven
4. Once oven is up to hot, slide pizza unto hot tray and let bake for 
5. Bake for about 20-30 min, the actual time depends on your oven and the pizza it self.
6. Once done, remove from oven and let rest a little on a wired surface, or cut immediately
7. Enjoy

> [!info] Tips
> 1. If a using a pizza peel is out of the question, shape pizza on parchment paper, grab the hot tray with a heat-proof glove and slide the parchment paper with the completed pizza unto tray. 
> 
> 2.  The dough is quite manuale and will break or fold if lifted by hand. Always support it on something flat while moving it around
> 3. See [[Pizza Toppings|pizza toppings]] for a list pizzas
