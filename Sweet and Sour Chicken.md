#Asian #Chicken #Pinaple 
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients (4 people I think)
___
**Marinade**
- [ ] 6 Chicken thighs (cut in 3 cm bits)
- [ ] 3 Garlic cloves
- [ ] Knob of Ginger
- [ ] 1 Egg
- [ ] 30 g Sake (very optional)
- [ ] 15 g Soy Sauce
- [ ] 10 g Vinegar
- [ ] 10 g Salt

**Batter**
- [ ] 1 part potato starch
- [ ] 2 parts flour 

**Sauce**
- [ ] 1 dl Pineapple Juice
- [ ] 1 dl Water
- [ ] 2 Tsp Sugar
- [ ] 2 Tsp Ketchup
- [ ] 2 Tsp Sweet Chili sauce
- [ ] 2 Tsp Vinegar
- [ ] 2 Tsp Soy Sauce
- [ ] 2 Tsp potato-starch

**Additional**
- [ ] 250 g rice (before cooked)
- [ ] Spring onion
## Walkthrough
___
**Chicken**
1. Mix marinade ingredients thoroughly and massage the chicken with it to coat the pieces well
2. Let rest for 30 min (start preparing the other things like veggies, sauce, and setting up a workspace for frying)
4. Prepare bowl with flour and cornstarch while heating the frying oil
6. Once hot, dip the marinated chicken in the frying oil until golden
7. Take out and let rest on a wire-rack
8. Once all chicken is fried, double fry every piece to make sure no moisture is on the outside
9. Remove the oil from heat (it's still very hot[^1])

**Sauce**
1. Add sauce ingredients to a pot and whisk to remove starch lumps
2. Heat up the pot on medium high and stir to prevent burning
3. When thick, remove from heat.

**Finishing**
1. Add veggies of choice to a large pan ("traditionally" bell peppers and maybe brocoli) and stir fry them
2. Once the veggies are done, add the fried chicken to the pan along with the thickened sauce and some spring onions. Mix together and serve with cooked rice

> [!Info] info
> 1. Never ever leave the oil while it's hot. Make sure any handle is not in a position where it can be knocked around, and don't ever leave the hot oil unattended!
> 2. Overcrowding the oil means large temperature drops, and possibly bubbling over. Fry in small batches of about 6 pieces at a time.
> 3. Don't fry in olive oil obviously
> 4. Don't cook this dish in a non-stick or carbon-steel pot or pan, the acidity from the pineapple will strip the top layer.


___


[^1] Frying oil gets to about 325$\degree$C
