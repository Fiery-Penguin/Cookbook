#Asian #Coconut #Mushrooms #Chicken 
[[Cookbook|Return]] 
## Ingredients
___
- [x] 250 g Chicken, in bits
- [ ] Fish-sauce?
- [x] 250 g Mushrooms, sliced
- [ ] 3 Carrots, sliced
- [ ] Lemongrass
- [x] 3 Knobs of Ginger
- [x] 3 Cloves of Garlic
- [x] Juice from 1/2 of a lime
- [ ] Fresh Coriander
- [x] 2 Chicken stock cubes
- [x] 1 Can of Coconut milk (400 g)
- [x] Dash of paprika
- [x] Dash of cayenne pepper
## Walkthrough
___
1. In a pot, add 1 litre of water, the stock cubes, garlic and ginger. Let boil for 15 minutes 
2. Remove the garlic and ginger from the pot after they've infused their flavour.
3. Add the mushrooms, carrots, chicken, coconut milk, spices and lime.
4. Let simmer for 30 minutes to cook the chicken and let the soup reduce a little bit.
5. Once done, kill the heat, cut some coriander for garnish and serve over noodles.
