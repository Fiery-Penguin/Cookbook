#Recipe/Spanish #Egg #Potato 
[[Cookbook|Return]]
## Ingredients (For 4 servings)
___
- [ ] 800 g Potato, cut into thin slices
- [ ] 75 g oil *(traditionally olive oil, it's quite expensive though)*
- [ ] 4 eggs *(about 200g)*
- [ ] 2 large onion, cut into thin slices
## Walkthrough
___
1. Whisk eggs, salt and pepper and set aside
2. Heat oil on a pan
3. Add potatoes to pan and fry until golden while flipping occasionally
4. Remove potatoes while leaving the oil
5. Fry the onions while stirring occasionally to avoid burning the onions
6. Remove the onions and discard the remaining oil *(about half)*
7. Add potatoes, onions and egg to a bowl, mix gently and pour back into pan
8. Once the top is set, flip the omelette using an upside down plate
9. Cook for another few minutes and remove from pan
